Product Segment

Drilling

We offer a comprehensive range of product for the upstream sector. This includes tools and equipment for Drilling, Completion, Well Pressure Control and Well Intervention.

Completion & Production

We offer an extensive line of Gas Lift, Rod Lift and Plunger Lift equipment. Our Gas Lift Division manufactures a variety of valves for both injection pressure and production pressure operation.

Artificial Lift

Our product offering for the Midstream and Downstream business includes Pipeline Pigs, Pipe and Pipeline, Fittings and Flanges, Pipe and Flange Protection, Valves and Seals.

Well Intervention

Our product offering for the Midstream and Downstream business includes Pipeline Pigs, Pipe and Pipeline, Fittings and Flanges, Pipe and Flange Protection, Valves and Seals.
