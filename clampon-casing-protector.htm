Clamp On Casing Protectors

REVATA Clamp-on Casing Protectors are manufactured from high grade UV stable Polyurethane that ensures high Abrasion, Impact & Wear resistance during heavy usage. They are bonded on special steel cage for a greater grip force.

The Clamp-On Casing Protector is attached to the Pin end of the Tubular using the Quick Fit & Release Lever mechanism. This enables easy installation and removal. In case the Clamp-On Protector is marginally large or small for the thread being serviced, a special tension adjustment screw enables user to increase or decrease the ID of the protector to achieve the perfect thread squeeze. Once the Clamp-On Protector is clamped onto the tubular threads, the Locking Lever is concealed in the Protector body to prevent damage or unintended release.

The hook shaped Locking Lever enables easy return of the Clamp-On Protector from the Rig floor to the pipe rack. Once the Locking Lever is opened, it can be hooked over a catch line and slid downwards to the Pipe rack.

Features:

  • REVATA Clamp-on Casing Protector eliminates damage to the tubulars threads while transportation and guarantee total threaded ends protection against corrosion and stresses that may occur during handling.

  • REVATA Clamp-on Casing Protector is made up of high grade Polyurethane that has excellent Abrasion, Impact & Wear resistance properties.

  • REVATA Clamp-on Casing Protectors has a strong steel cage molded into the body to provide high clamping force that is necessary to secure the protector onto the tubular threads.

  • REVATA Clamp-on Casing Protectors are available from 4 1/2" to 30" casing sizes
Hardness: 90-95 Shore A
