Revata Engineering’s Cast Steel Thread Protectors with lifting bails / Lift Nubbins offer the best thread protection with excellent lifting capabilities to ensure safe handling of Tubular Products, Drill Collars, Drill Pipes, Downhole Tooling’s etc during manufacturing, shipping and on the rigs.

The Cast Steel Thread Protectors with lifting bails / Lift Nubbins are manufactured from steel castings in accordance with A.S.T.M.S. No. A27 Grade 60-30 and B.S. 3100 Grade A1. They are heat treated to give 60,000 psi Tensile Strength and 30,000 psi Yield Strength. The heavy-duty bails are suitable for picking up and laying down a drill collars, drill pipes and other Bottom Hole Assembly components.

The Threads of the Cast Steel Thread Protectors with lifting bails / Lift Nubbins are precision machined to the requirements set by the A.P.I. Standards. Revata follows a stringent quality inspection procedure. Each lifting bail leaving our facility is 100% MPI tested and a random sample from each lot size is load tested to ensure an ultimate quality product is delivered to the customer.

Features and Benefits of the Cast Steel Thread Protectors with Lifting Bails / Lift Nubbins:

  • We affix a Unique Serial Number on each Protector for complete traceability.
  • Each protector is load tested and certified and all necessary documents in this regard are provided.

Each Cast Steel Thread Protector with lifting bail / Lift Nubbin has been load tested and inspected prior to sale. Due to the rough nature in which drill collars are handled, Revata Engineering recommends that the end users carry out frequent inspection to ensure that the bail is not cracked, threads are not damaged and that proper fitting to the connection is possible.

Note: Cast Steel Thread Protectors with lifting bail / Lift Nubbins should only be used to pick up or lay down one drill collar at the same time.
